Student Sim

Tutorial

Your goal is to sucessfully get your student through 3 years of high school.

Manage their stats.

INT: How smart is your student? Increases training capability.
PHY: How athletic is your student? Slightly decreases hp loss.
CHR: How chrismatic is your student? Increases odds of making friends.
LUK: How lucky is your student? Increases odds of good events and bonuses.
HP: How healthy is your student? If it drops to 0, make sure to hold a funeral.

Instruct them on how to spend their time wisely.

Training, increase your stats!
Study: Spend some time hitting the books, get smarter!
Workout: Head to the gym, work out those muscles!
Act: Join the local theater and work on expression!
Pray: Go to church, maybe god will bless you!
Rest, restore your hp!
Read: Pull out a good story and chill.
Yoga: Clear your mind and get some stretches in.
Nothing: Stare into the void doing absolutely nothing.
Sleep: Nothing better than getting some zzz in.
Friends, develop bonds together!
Study: Why study alone when you can do it together?
Sports: Hop on the field, its time to game.
Chat: Chill and sip on some pipin hot coffee.
Hangout: Eat out, watch a movie, anything goes.

Overcome major events and graduate!

Special events:
Introduction ceremony: Start your highschool adventure.
Camp out: Go camping in your 2nd year summer with the school!
Prom: Party with friends on your last year.
Regular events:
Exams: Study up before November, Febuary, and June
Sports Day: Compete with your class every April
